Projects

ScrappyAI

ScrappyAI is a recipe assistant chatbot designed to help users transform leftover ingredients into meals. It enables users to save recipes, ask questions about cooking instructions, and modify recipes to accommodate dietary restrictions. Built using Python, Flask, and SQLite.

Instagram Clone

A fully functional social media platform inspired by Instagram. Users can create accounts, share posts with images and captions, like and comment on posts, follow and unfollow other users, and explore content in a dynamic feed. The app features smooth interactions like infinite scrolling, real-time updates for likes and comments, and a modern, responsive design.

Gro-Share-ies

A rideshare service that helps students get to and from grocery stores affordably. As part of an entrepreneurship program in Copenhagen, I worked with a team of five to develop a landing page, conduct user validation surveys, and design a mobile app prototype in Figma. We pitched the idea to J12 Ventures and Danish investors, seeking $80,000 in initial funding.
